The New Jersey State Future of Work Task Force needs to hear from you to understand your concerns about the impact of technology on the future of work in the Garden State.

We are focusing on three key themes:

Skills and Lifelong Learning
Worker Rights and Benefits
Worker Health and Safety

What We Are Doing

There is no doubt that technology will transform the way we work, the industries we work in, and the skills needed to succeed in the economy of tomorrow. To better prepare the State, its institutions, and its people for the future that technology will bring, we need to better understand how you experience the challenges of the future of work.

Between DATE X and DATE Y, the Future of Work Task Force wants to your feedback to help us define challenges associated with the future of work.

What We Will Do

Your insights and ideas are critical to our work!

At the end of stage one, the Task Force and its partners will have a rank-ordered list of the challenges that YOU think are most important. The Task Force will then use these insights to inform the drafting of its recommendations.
