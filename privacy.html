PRIVACY POLICY

The State of New Jersey respects your personal privacy. To see how the State of New Jersey protects your privacy rights, please visit: https://www.nj.gov/nj/privacy.html.

Be considerate of others’ privacy; do not reference another person without their permission.

Remember: if you post it, you own it.

Think twice before you publish. Would you want your family, friends, colleagues, or others to see the content you post? Keep in mind that even if original content can be deleted, content that is shared and distributed through an array of channels can be reposted, searched, and found on the Internet. Do not post your personal, confidential or proprietary information. By posting content to the Website, you agree to allow anyone with access to the Website to access it and use said content without restriction, except with regard to any applicable intellectual property rights that you or others may have in such content. By using this Website, you agree that you will not post or transmit material that is confidential or proprietary to you or a third-party.

If you feel that any User Generated Content violates the above rules or your rights, including your privacy, you should immediately contact us at support@smarter.nj.gov. You may also advise your supervisor, the Civil Service Commission and/or the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission.

By using this Website, you acknowledge that a Google Analytics, a third-party analytics provider, will report anonymized information about the locations of Website users, how long they spend on the Website, and which pages they visit in order to enable us improve this Website.
