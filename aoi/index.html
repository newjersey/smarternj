FUTURE OF WORK IN

Changes in technology and in the economy will affect the way we work, our workplaces, the jobs we work in, and the skills we will need to succeed. We want to know how workers are experiencing these changes already, how they believe technology will affect their ability to find and keep good employment in the future, and what kinds of training opportunities and workplace protections they need most.
