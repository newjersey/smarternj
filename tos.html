Using SmarterNJ

Welcome! Thank you for participating. Please note that everything you post on SmarterNJ will be accessible to the general public. Because of that, we want to ensure you keep the following in mind and review the full Terms and Conditions of Use and this site's Privacy Notice below.

Your use of this site should be guided by the following principles:

  • Be creative - do post content and ideas that are creative, thoughtful, and could help the State of New Jersey improve government efficiency and save taxpayer dollars.
  • Be aware - do not post content that is confidential, sensitive or proprietary, or that might violate copyright or intellectual property laws.
  • Be nice - do not post content that is profane, obscene, or otherwise hurtful or harmful to others.
  • Be respectful of the privacy of others - do not post content about other people unless you have asked for their permission to do so.
  • Be informed - by using this site, know that you are agreeing to the site's Terms and Conditions of Use, Information Collection Practices, and Privacy Notice. Make sure to read the full Terms and Conditions of Use and this site's Privacy Notice and Information Collection Practices below.

And if you see anything on the site that violates the letter or spirit of these rules, please contact us at support@smarter.nj.gov.
